Request Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your MathQuest account and all associated data by sending us an email. We will process your request within 30 days.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account will remove all your progress, XP, streaks, and lesson history. This cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All lesson progress and completion history
  • XP points, streaks, and achievements
  • Subscription records (cancellation handled by Google Play)

How to request deletion

Send an email to krislove79@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My MathQuest Account" and include the email address linked to your account. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.
